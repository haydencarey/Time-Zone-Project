Who's Asleep and Who's Awake

Click here for documentation

Here's an-easy-to-use application that can determine if someone is asleep or awake depending on their location
Simply use the map below or input the latitude and longitude coordinates of your desired locatoin.

Some notes:

  1. From 8 am to 6 pm is "Awake, and working hours."
    From 6 pm to 11 pm is Awake, but Outside Working Hours.
    From 11 pm to 8 am is Asleep
  2. Choose a city from the drop down.
  3. If you're looking for a location not listed, look up the latitude and longitude coordinates of your desired location.
  4. The coordinates should be in this format: 41.40338, 22.17403.
  5. If you see a degree symbol, use the symbol as a decimal spot.
  6. A south latitude coordinate is negative. A west longitude coordinate is negative.
